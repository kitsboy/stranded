Executive Summary — Turning Wasted Energy into Verifiable Wealth

We map every site, integrate real generator models (6+ methane-to-power machines with specs, CAPEX, efficiency), and compute honest per-site Value/ROI (generator CapEx + ASIC mining with live BTC + explicit methane loss opportunity cost + financing). One of the planet’s highest-impact climate + capital opportunities.

The Paradox: Billions of dollars of extractable energy and some of the world’s most potent greenhouse gas emissions sit in a country with world-class data, abundant capital, and no shortage of technological sophistication. What has been missing is the bridge.
Why Now: Public ECCC data + Bitcoin mining as the perfect offtaker + synchronized capital appetite (Canadian, CETA €200B+, Bitcoin-native, traditional PE). We have built the transparent platform that makes these opportunities visible, modelable, and fundable.
