## Why We Exist

The paradox at the heart of the modern energy system is striking and persistent:

Billions of dollars of extractable energy sits in verified locations across Canada—right now—while the same resource generates some of the planet's most potent greenhouse gas emissions. The data exists. The technology exists. The capital exists.

**What has been missing is the bridge.**

For decades, stranded energy (methane venting, curtailed renewables, industrial waste heat, and more) was invisible to capital markets because three things were true:

1. **The opportunities were fragmented and hard to find.** No unified, transparent view existed.
2. **The economics were impossible to model.** Without a path to sell the power, ROI calculations were theoretical at best.
3. **No scalable offtaker existed for remote, small-scale, or irregular power.** Traditional utilities couldn't justify the infrastructure. Industrial users couldn't use it. So the energy was wasted.

Bitcoin mining changed the third constraint. Suddenly, there was a buyer for stranded power that didn't require geographic proximity, grid connection, or predictable, large-scale supply. It was location-agnostic, economically rational, and rapidly deployable.

**Stranded** was built to solve the first two constraints—to make these opportunities visible and modelable at a scale that attracts the capital they deserve.

We exist because:
- The climate math demands the highest-leverage actions, and stranded energy capture is among the highest.
- The economics work, and should work for everyone involved—landowners, operators, investors, and communities.
- The data exists, and belongs in the hands of those who can act on it.
- Capital is ready, but has been waiting for clarity, not hype.

## What Success Looks Like

Success for Stranded is not measured in press releases or funding rounds. It's measured in:

**Energy Captured**  
Every verified stranded energy site in Canada (and beyond) that can be profitably developed is being developed. The waste stops.

**Capital Deployed**  
Billions flow efficiently from federal programs, provincial incentives, European trade agreements, Bitcoin-native sources, and traditional infrastructure capital into the highest-impact projects.

**Communities Strengthened**  
Landowners, First Nations, and local communities understand the value on their lands and capture meaningful revenue from it. Energy transition becomes an economic opportunity, not an external imposition.

**The Platform Effect**  
Stranded becomes the default starting point for anyone serious about stranded energy project origination, evaluation, or funding. Not because we own the data (we don't), but because our tools are the clearest, most honest, and most useful in the world.

**New Category**  
"Stranded energy" stops being a niche environmental problem and becomes a recognized, liquid, investable asset class with its own funding mechanisms, investor base, and operator ecosystem.

**Connect capital efficiently.** We believe the bottleneck is not technology or data—it's friction between opportunities and capital. We are obsessed with removing that friction.

**Move with urgency and craft.** This is frontier work. We move fast, but we build carefully. The best partners and capital are drawn to both speed and thoughtfulness.
