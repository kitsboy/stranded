## The Problem (And It's Bigger Than You Think)

**Every day, Canada vents 19 million cubic meters of natural gas worth billions of dollars.**

This is stranded methane. It's not an edge case or a minor inefficiency. It's one of the planet's most potent climate crises—methane is 25× more powerful than CO₂ as a greenhouse gas—and simultaneously one of the largest unmonetized energy resources available.

The sites are real. The data is verified. The technology to capture it exists.

**What's been missing: a way to make it actually investable.**

Until now.

## The Stranded Platform (Stranded Value)

We built the world's most sophisticated public intelligence system for turning wasted energy into revenue — now with real generator models, per-site Value/ROI wired to the live 2,611-site dataset (emission → power kW/CapEx → BTC revenue + methane loss + financing), home business flow, persona paths, and a full Education Center with simulators + configurator.

### 📊 Economics Engine (Bitcoin-First)
Honest, sophisticated ROI modeling that includes what everyone else ignores: pool fees, maintenance, uptime, equipment degradation, realistic network assumptions. Modeling in BTC, EUR, CAD, or USD. No hype. Just numbers.

### ⭐ Stranded Score™
Our proprietary algorithm identifies the best opportunities instantly. It doesn't optimize for media attention or "vibes"—it surfaces the sites with the highest combination of capture potential, accessibility, and economics.

### 🎯 Portfolio Simulator
Model entire scenarios. See daily profit, CO₂ impact, and payback timelines across projects. Export briefs for operators, investors, and governments.

### 🔍 Full Transparency
Every dataset attribute is inspectable. Every scoring decision is documented. Every model assumption is visible. We believe capital follows clarity, not obscurity.
